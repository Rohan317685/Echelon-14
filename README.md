Echelon‑14
A top‑secret blinky‑board prototype engineered by a covert team of 25 specialists

Born inside a sealed laboratory and funded by an eye‑watering budget of experimental R&D, Project Echelon‑14 was never meant to see daylight. After years of failed iterations, unstable power cores, and classified field tests, the prototype finally reached operational status.

But everything changed when an unidentified team member leaked the encrypted schematics.
Now the once‑classified device is fully open‑source, released into the wild for anyone bold and brave enough to build it.
